About Me

Hello! I am a software engineer based in Emeryville, CA, with experience in
building efficient and scalable end to end web applications.

I graduated from UC Santa Barbara in June 2023 with a Bachelors of Science
in Data Science and Statistics. In 2024, I decided to pursue a long-standing goal
by enrolling in App Academy, a rigorous software engineering bootcamp.
Through this program, I deepened my knowledge of programming languages and
mastered a wide range of technologies essential for full-stack web development.

My passion for this field goes beyond web applications. I am always eager to
take on new projects and continue building on my skills. I'm excited to continue
learning and exploring emerging technologies, while focusing on building
user-centric applications that address real-world challenges. My ultimate goal is
to contribute to impactful products that make a positive global impact.

Outside of programming, I enjoy discovering new restaurants, spending time in
nature, and pursuing creative hobbies like crocheting. Whether in my personal
hobbies or professional work, I value experiences that foster creativity
and encourage innovation.
